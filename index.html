iOS Ecosystem

Access the App Store and download "8 Ball Pool" by Miniclip. Ensure your device is running iOS 14+. Registration requires a verified Apple or Facebook ID for global rank tracking.

Android / Huawei

Download via Play Store or AppGallery. Requires Google Play Games integration for tournament lobby access.

PC Web Client

Run exclusively via Miniclip.com. Guest accounts are restricted from the Season 1 roster.
